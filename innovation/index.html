Case Studies

Innovation takes initiative

Emirates Islamic, one of the leading Islamic financial institutions in the UAE, has won the 'Best Digital Innovation in Islamic Banking' award at MEA Finance Banking Technology Summit & Awards, in recognition of its commitment to service excellence and digitization.

WhatsApp Banking

Banking is now just a chat away

We’re constantly striving to make banking easier for you. That’s why we launched WhatsApp banking service, becoming the first Islamic bank in the world to do so. This service further enhances Emirates Islamic’ s suite of digital financial products, enabling customers to conduct daily banking activities in a seamless and hassle-free manner.

Smart Pass

Digital Banking transactions are now easier and even more secure than ever before With Smart Pass, we took digital banking security to a new level by replacing SMS authentication codes to transact via Mobile & Online banking.

InstaPay

Go cashless with InstaPay

We know how important your time is to you, so we do what we can to give some back to you. Transfer money in seconds to anyone within Emirates Islamic.

Tablet Banking

Emirates Islamic launched its first paper-less solution whereby branches can source current & savings account STP solution for all nationalities via tablet banking solution.

ITM (Interactive teller machines)

Emirates Islamic launched ITMs to offer convenient banking and payment solutions that will allow customers to seamlessly carry out a variety of transactions such as cheque encashment, deposit, withdrawal.

Expo 2020 Dubai

Our vision for the future of banking

At Expo 2020 Dubai, we showcased our pioneering vision for the future of global banking,
championing inclusion, sustainability, and new possibilities.

Spanning approximately 3,000 sq ft, The Future Banking space comprised five pods each
housing deeply immersive, interactive, and engaging exhibits showcasing the role that banks will
play in fulfilling a customer’s future needs, aspirations, and goals.

The innovative concept-driven exhibits enabled millions of visitors from across the globe to
experience different virtual personas and learn about cutting-edge banking initiatives that aim to
address future challenges.

Key Milestones

2021

Mobile App - the Bank’s Mobile App continued to be among the highest rated apps in the financial industry, with an outstanding rating of 4.5/5 from more than 45,000 users on the Apple App Store

Instant Account Opening for UAE Nationals via Tablet Banking - Since Emirates Islamic’ s goal is as part of its strategy of being the bank of choice for UAE Nationals, Emirates Islamic launched a fully straight-through processing (STP) solution to source Current and Saving Accounts for UAE Nationals via tablet banking at Emirates Islamic branches, – reducing the turn-around times from two days to 15 minutes.

2020

Partnership - Emirates Islamic became the First Islamic bank in the region to partner with Huawei to launch Emirates Islamic app in Huawei App

First Teller-Less Branch (Interactive Teller Machine)- First teller-less branch was launched in Mirdif City Centre Branch with interactive teller machines

App Demo feature on mobile banking -
