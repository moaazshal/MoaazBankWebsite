Success stories

Innovation takes initiative

Interactive Teller Machine

Emirates Islamic launched its first Interactive Teller Machine (ITM) which is used to avail banking services right from opening an account to carrying out banking transactions, transfers, etc.

Tablet Banking

Emirates Islamic launched its first paper-less solution whereby branches can source current & savings account STP solution for all nationalities via tablet banking solution.

WhatsApp Banking

Banking is now just a chat away

We’re constantly striving to make banking easier for you. That’s why we launched WhatsApp banking service, becoming the first Islamic bank in the world to do so. This service further enhances Emirates Islamic’ s suite of digital financial products, enabling customers to conduct daily banking activities in a seamless and hassle-free manner.
