Emirates Islamic Cashback Plus Credit Card brings to you:

  • Up to 10% cash back on supermarkets, education, dining and telecom spends in the UAE and abroad
  • An embedded RTA NOL chip that allows you to make payments on the RTA transit network
  • Auto top-up facility for Salik and NOL accounts
  • Gold Class cabin access on the metro
  • Unlimited complimentary access to over 1000 airport lounges worldwide

Please note all above benefits can be enjoyed by Primary as well as Supplementary card holders

An Emirates Islamic Cashback Plus Credit Card is the only credit card that brings to you a cash back equipped rewards program with an embedded RTA Nol Chip. This makes it a unique offering in the market as the Card not only will rewards you for your daily spends but also allows you to make RTA network payments through a single card. You can earn cash back on both domestic and international spend under each qualifying categories. It also allows you to enjoy complimentary unlimited access to over 1000 airport lounges worldwide.

Cash back will be earned on payments for supermarket, school fees, dining and telecom earn up to 10% cash back in the form of cash back points. The maximum cash back mentioned above is subject to meeting the minimum monthly expenditure of AED3,000. Total expenditure includes all spends on your Card and are not limited to payments made for supermarket, school fees, dining and telecom only.

Cash back % is based on the spend tiers. Please see the below table for your reference. Under each category there is a cap on the maximum cashback that can be earned.
