What is Smart Pass?

Emirates Islamic Smart Pass is a transaction authorisation method in our mobile banking app which ensures more security and convenience for Online & Mobile Banking transactions. With this new level of security, you do not need to wait for SMS Authentication Codes anymore to authorise your transactions. Smart Pass enables you to authorise your transactions with Smart Pass PIN which you define in the Smart Pass activation process.

How can I start using Smart Pass?

To start using Smart Pass, you need to activate Smart Pass in your Emirates Islamic Bank Mobile Banking App. For activation, go to Smart Pass menu on your Emirates Islamic Mobile Banking App and follow the instructions on the screen.

Is there any fee for using Smart Pass?

Smart Pass is a free of charge service. Data fee due to utilisation and activation of Smart Pass will be charged by your operator.

Can I use Smart Pass and SMS authentication code together?

No, you cannot use Smart Pass and SMS Authentication Code together. When you activate Smart Pass, you will be authorising your transactions by entering your Smart Pass PIN. To use SMS Authentication Code, you need to deactivate your Smart Pass on your device.

How to resent Smart Pass Pin?

If you forgot / blocked your Smart Pass PIN, you need to re-activate your Smart Pass through Smart Pass menu on your Emirates Islamic Mobile Banking App. You can re-activate your Smart Pass PIN by selecting Forgot PIN / Re-activate.

How to deactivate Smart Pass?

You can deactivate your Smart Pass through the Emirates Islamic Mobile Banking App, Online Banking or Phone Banking.

To deactivate through Emirates Islamic Mobile Banking App:

  • Smart Pass Menu
  • Enter your Smart Pass PIN
  • Select Deactivation

To deactivate through Online Banking:
  • Log in to Online Banking
  • Select Smart Pass menu
  • Select Deactivation
  • Enter the Smart Pass token generated by entering your Smart Pass PIN on your mobile device, and then type it on the Online Banking screen.

How do I deactivate Smart Pass if my device is lost or stolen?

If your phone is stolen or lost you can deactivate your Smart Pass via phone banking. Simply call 600 599 995 and connect to an agent for deactivation.

If I change my device, how can I use Smart Pass on my new device?

If you change your device, you will need to configure Smart Pass on your new device and the one on your old device will be deactivated automatically.

Can I use Smart Pass on Multiple devices?

No. Smart Pass can be activated on only one mobile device or tablet.

How do I download the Emirates Islamic Mobile Banking App?

You can download the Emirates Islamic Bank App to a supported device directly from Apple iTunes App Store or Google Play, depending on the smartphone you are using.

Is there any charge to download the Emirates Islamic Bank Mobile App?

This mobile app is free to download.

Can I use online mobile banking while roaming?

You can enjoy the service as long as you have Wi-Fi connectivity or subscribed to roaming data service through your operator. Data roaming services are chargeable services which you may encounter additional charges to your phone bills.

How many queuing tickets can I avail via the Mobile Banking App?

You can avail only 1 queuing ticket at a time. Once your first ticket is announced, then you can issue another ticket.

Can I open an account through Mobile Banking, even if I do not hold an existing Emirates Islamic account?

No. To open a new account through mobile app, you have to hold an existing Emirates Islamic account.
