FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Listed below are the commonly asked questions relating to our products and offerings.


Priority Banking:

Who is a Priority Banking Customer?

Customers who meet the following criteria qualify:

  • Priority Banking: Minimum of AED 100,000 as monthly average balance across deposits and investments, or Salary Transfer of AED 20,000-40,000 or Home Finance of AED 1M - 2.5 M

How do you recognize or identify a Priority customer?

  • Priority Banking Customers can be identified by Platinum MasterCard Debit Card with "Priority" written on it.
  • These customers also have Priority Banking branded cheque books.

What privileges are Priority customers entitled to?

Priority Clients:

  • Dedicated team of virtual relationship managers to assist you with all your queries, complaints and service requests
  • Complementary access to airport lounges across the middle east including the Marhaba lounges at Dubai Airport Terminals 1,2 & 3
  • 24 hour emergency assistance from MasterCard by calling 001-636-722-7111
  • Preferential Pricing on banking products
  • 30 free teller transactions in a month vs. 6 free for Classic/select accounts
  • No charges on inward foreign currency DD, Cheque or TT
  • Tailor-made Internet and Mobile Banking
  • Priority on teller queue using the Priority Gold ATM cards
  • Access to dedicated Priority Banking call center 80011122

How is Priority Banking different?

We enhance customer experience by servicing them holistically through understanding their needs, tastes & preferences; appreciate their financial goals and objectives to deliver banking solutions and lifestyle experiences that seem to be truly personalized. A Priority customer can expect transparent and convenient service, along with sound Investment & Financial advice. More so, they can expect prompt & clear response to their queries combined with regular communication.

How to change existing account to Priority?

Call us @800 111 22 or visit any of our branches and we will switch your account to Priority if you qualify. Your existing debit and credit cards will also be upgraded on request.


Priority Gold Banking:

Who is a Priority Gold Customer?

Customers who meet the following criteria qualify:

  • Priority Banking: Minimum of AED 250,000 as monthly average balance across deposits and investments, or Salary Transfer of AED 40,000 or more or Home Finance of more than AED 2.5 M

How do you recognize or identify a Priority Gold customer?

  • Priority Gold customers can be identified by the World MasterCard debit card with "Priority Gold" written on it.
  • These customers also have Priority Gold branded cheque books.

What privileges are Priority Gold customers entitled to?

    Priority Gold Clients:

  • Exclusive priority banking centers (over 26) in UAE
  • Dedicated team of Relationship Managers and customer service representatives to provide the customers with hassle free servicing
  • Complimentary World MasterCard Debit Card
  • Pre-approved Skywards Platinum Credit Card with an option to upgrade to Skywards Infinite card
  • Preferential pricing on banking products
  • Wealth Management Services through our dedicated set of investment advisors
  • 60 free teller transactions in a month vs. 6 free for Classic/select accounts
  • No charges on inward foreign currency DD, Cheque or TT
  • Free cheque book
  • Tailor made Internet and Mobile Banking
  • Priority on teller queue using the Priority Gold ATM cards
  • Designated Parking Slots
  • Access to dedicated Priority Banking call center 80011122
  • Value Added Lifestyle Benefits
    • Access to Airport Service/Lounges across the middle east
    • Discounts at Jumeirah Hotels
    • Exclusive World Privilege Access Programme
    • Complimentary MasterCard Hertz Car Rental Gold Membership where you can avail up to 15% discount for participating Hertz locations worldwide and up to 15% discount for Hertz Prestige, Fun, Family and Green Collections

How is Priority Gold Banking different?

Emirates Islamic's Priority Gold has been created to serve our prestigious members through building relationships based on integrity, dedication and mutual trust. You can expect sophisticated advice through dedicated Relationship Manager and Customer Service Representatives who will take care of all your day-to-day banking needs including sophisticated financial advice

How to change existing account to Priority Gold?

Call us @800 111 22 or visit any of our branches. We will switch your account to Priority Gold if you qualify. Your debit and credit cards will also be upgraded on request.
