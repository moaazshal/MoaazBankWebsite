Emirates Islamic Chat Banking via WhatsApp is an easy to use secure channel which allows you to carry out basic banking functions on the go.   • Last 5 transactions

Security Tips for Subscribers

  • To ensure that you are talking to Emirates Islamic on WhatsApp, look for the green badge next to Emirates Islamic's name which confirms that this is a verified business account.
  • Data transfer happens via a secure channel between your device, WhatsApp and Emirates Islamic. The information is encrypted and cannot be seen by anyone.
  • Emirates Islamic will never ask you to share your personal information or account/ card numbers via WhatsApp. Depending on your enquiry, you will be asked to input only the last 4 digits of your card or account number. If someone does ask you for it, they do not represent the Bank and you should not under any circumstance provide this information.
  • Do not reply to WhatsApp messages that request your personal information. They may appear to come from a trusted friend or business, but they are designed to trick you into disclosing sensitive personal information.
  • You can only subscribe to Emirates Islamic Chat Banking via your registered mobile number with the bank. The same number has to be used in WhatsApp.
  • Lock your mobile device when not in use to prevent unauthorized access to your information.
  • Never share your device.
  • Activate your SIM card with a PIN.
  • Enable WhatsApp Two-Step Verification under Settings/ Account/ Two-Step Verification: with any attempt to verify your mobile number on WhatsApp must be accompanied by a 6-digit PIN you create.

How to use Emirates Islamic Chat Banking via WhatsApp?

We are still learning to understand you better and currently respond best to keywords.

  • Type 'Balance', 'Bal' to check the balance of your Current or Savings Account
  • Type 'Card transactions XXXX' where 'XXXX' are the last 4 digits of your Card number, to get the last 5 transactions of your Card
  • Type 'Account transactions XXXX' where 'XXXX' are the last 4 digits of your Account number, to get the last 5 transactions of your Account
  • Type 'Balance cc XXXX' where XXXX are the last 4 digits of your Credit Card number, to know the outstanding balance on your Credit Card
  • Type 'Block XXXX' or 'Unblock XXXX' where 'XXXX' are the last 4 digits of your Card number, to block / unblock your Debit or Credit Card temporarily
  • Type 'Cheque book XXXX' where 'XXXX' are the last 4 digits of your account number, to request for your new cheque book
  • Type 'FX', to find out the current foreign exchange rates of select countries.
  • Type ‘Credit Limit XXXX’ where 'XXXX' are the last 4 digits of your Credit Card.
  • Type ‘SRREQ-1-XXXXX/SREI-1-XXXX’ where ‘XXXXXX’ is the reference number.
