Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for the Emirates Islamic Prepaid Card you have to follow this simple process.

  • Sign up: apply by visiting your nearest Emirates Islamic branch
  • Activate: once you receive the card, you can generate a PIN by contacting our call centre 600 599 995
  • Ready for use : now load and reload your card as per your convenience at any EI branch cash deposit machine or simply through your online banking account

  • Set up your ATM PIN by calling our Contact Centre at 600 599 995
  • Select the option for Activation of your Prepaid Card
  • Select the option for not having Authorization Code
  • Wait for our Contact Centre agent to verify your account, who will send you an Authorization Code
  • Call our Contact Centre again after you receive the Authorization Code on your mobile
  • Select the option for Activation of your Prepaid Card
  • Select the option for having Authorization Code
  • Input the information as requested
  • Setup your PIN and start using the card!

  • Recharge your Prepaid Visa Card at any Emirates Islamic branch, through Online Banking, or at any of our CDMs
  • While recharging through the CDM or through Online Banking you will need to also use your Savings Account number that is printed on your card
  • While submitting cash through the CDM you can either insert your card or input the Account Number for the cash top up to work

To withdraw cash you will need to use the ATM PIN you have set up and then select the option stating Savings Account and then select the amount you want to withdraw.

If you are not currently subscribed to Online Banking, you can easily register by visiting your nearest Emirates Islamic branch.

If you are already subscribed to Online Banking, you can use the option for Adding New Account in the Profile Information tab on the Online Banking menu.

3D Secure Service is an additional layer of security you can activate by simply following the steps provided during the payment process you will have one ID and one Password which you should remember and use each time you conduct an online transaction and the website requests this information from you. This will provide extra security and protection for your Prepaid Card and safeguard you against fraudulent usage of the card. Please do not share either the ID or the password with anyone else.
