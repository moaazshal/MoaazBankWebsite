How to set up Apple Pay

Enjoy a faster and easier way. Simply add your Emirates Islamic Credit Card, Debit Card or Prepaid Card to Apple Pay Wallet.

To set-up, follow these easy steps:

iPhone:

  • Open the Apple Pay Wallet app.
  • Tap the '+' sign in the upper-right corner
  • Use your camera to capture your Emirates Islamic Credit card, Debit Card or Prepaid Card details and follow the on-screen instructions
  • Set your Emirates Islamic Card as your default card*

iPad:

  • Go to Settings.
  • Tap "Wallet & Apple Pay"
  • Select "Add Credit or Debit Card" & follow on-screen instructions
  • Set your Emirates Islamic Card as your default card*

Apple Watch:

  • Open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone.
  • Tap "Wallet & Apple Pay"
  • Select "Add Credit or Debit Card" and follow on-screen instructions
  • Set your Emirates Islamic Card as your default card*

Set your Emirates Islamic Credit Card, Debit Card or Prepaid Card as your default Card in Apple Pay Wallet:

  • Go to Settings.
  • Click on "Wallet & Apple Pay"
  • Tap "Default Card"
  • Select your Emirates Islamic Card

Apple Pay is available only on Apple devices iPhone, Apple Watch, iPad, and Mac. And iOS version 11 and above.

View the terms and conditions by clicking here.

How to pay using Apple Pay

Paying with Apply Pay is quick and simple. After your Emirates Islamic Credit card, Debit Card or Prepaid Card is added to Apple Pay Wallet, just visit the stores where Apple Pay is accepted and follow the instructions given below.

Paying in stores:

There’s no need to wake your iPhone or open an app. Just follow this simple process:

  • Hold the phone near a reader with your finger on the Touch ID
  • You will see “Done” on the display, feel a subtle vibration and hear a beep
  • Double tap your card and then select ‘Set as Default Card’

This is the process to pay with an Apple Watch:

  • Double-click the side button and hold the display of your Apple Watch up to the reader
  • You will feel a gentle tap and hear a beep confirming that your payment information was sent

Paying in apps and on the web:

To pay in apps and on the web with iPhone or iPad:

  • Hold the phone near a reader with your finger on the Touch ID
  • You will see “Done” on the display, feel a subtle vibration and hear a beep
  • Double tap your card and then select ‘Set as Default Card’

To pay on Mac in Safari choose Apple Pay then complete the purchase with your iPhone or Apple Watch

Where is Apple Pay accepted?

Supermarkets, hotels, restaurants as well as retail outlets and countless other locations, you can use Apple Pay everywhere contactless payments are accepted.

Just look for one of these symbols at checkout.

You can also use Apple Pay within some of the most popular apps in the App Store.
