Frequently Asked Questions

1. When will I get the statement credit of AED 200/-?

AED 200/- will be credited on meeting the spend criteria of AED 10,000 within 60 days from the card issuance date.

2. Once I meet the spend criteria, within how many days will the amount be credited into my credit card?

Once the spend criteria is met, AED 200/- will be credited within 30 days.

3. What happens if I miss the spend criteria?

If you don't meet the spend criteria, then you will not be eligible for the statement credit.

4. What happens if miss the 60 days criteria?

If you miss meeting the spend criteria within 60 days from the card issuance date, then you will not be eligible for the statement credit of AED 200/-.

1. How much cashback will I earn on Fuel spends, in a month?

You will earn 10% cashback on Fuel spends, capped to AED 100 per month.

2. How do I redeem the cashback earned through Fuel spends?

It will be automatically credited to your credit card account. Cashback will reflect in the next month's statement.

3. Is there any spend condition, to earn the 10% cashback on fuel spends?

There is no minimum spend criteria on the earning the cashback.

4. Do I earn EI SmartMiles as well for Fuel spends?

No. For Fuel Spends, you will only earn cashback points and not EI SmartMiles.

1. How do I login to EI SmartMiles Portal?

You need to register yourself on the portal. Registration is a very simple, two-step process:

  • Step 1: Visit www.eirewards.ae, click on 'Activate' and enter your loyalty account number, Date of Birth and Name on your Credit card.
  • Step 2: Activate your account by setting up your login id and password. We recommend that you take a few minutes to browse the portal to discover the power of EI SmartMiles.

2. Where do I find my EI SmartMiles Loyalty number?

It will be mentioned on the face of your credit card, below your name.

3. How does Instant Purchase work?

Instant Purchase allows you to redeem your EI SmartMiles from any outlet or online site in the world. All you have to do is use your EI SmartMiles against your next purchase and pay for your chosen item with the Emirates Islamic Expo Credit Card. Before making the payment enable the Instant Purchase feature in your EI SmartMiles online or mobile app account and then make a purchase at your chosen shop or online site, for example buy your morning coffee with your EI SmartMiles for as little as 2,000 miles. After 5 days the EI SmartMiles amount will be deducted from your account and a statement credit will be posted onto your Expo Credit Card.

4. What purchases are defined as Expo & Emaar outlet spends?

Purchases made at Expo & Emaar Retail outlets will be defined based on the merchant Ids. Few of the Emaar Retail outlets are Dubai Mall, Marina Mall, Emaar Entertainment.

5. On Birthdays, how many EI SmartMiles will I earn?

On Birthdays, customers will earn 5 EI SmartMiles per AED 1 spend. For example:

  • Example 1: On Emaar spends customers will earn 5(3+2) EI SmartMiles
  • Example 2: On supermarket spends customers will earn 5(0.3+4.7) EI SmartMiles
Please note the incremental EI SmartMiles will be credited separately as a bonus in your EI SmartMiles account.

6. What is the maximum EI SmartMiles I can earn in a month?

In any given month, a Card member shall only be entitled to earn EI SmartMiles for transactions carried out up to the extent of the assigned Credit Limit on the Card. Transactions exceeding the assigned Credit Limit shall not be entitled to earn EI SmartMiles. ATM transactions, balance transfers, cash-on-call, installments, and cash advance are not eligible for EI SmartMiles earning.

1. How much cashback will I earn on Expo ticket purchase?

Emirates Islamic EXPO cardholders earn a 50% cashback on the first EXPO 2020 Dubai ticket transaction. Maximum cashback a cardholder can earn is up to AED 350. Cashback will be credited in the form of EI SmartMiles.
For Example:

  • Example 1: If you buy a ticket worth AED 450, you will get AED 225 back (in the form of 22,500 EI SmartMiles).
  • Example 1: If you buy a ticket worth AED 450, you will get AED 225 back (in the form of 22,500 EI SmartMiles). However, if you do another transaction of AED 400, then you will not earn any cashback, as it is applicable only on the first transaction.

2. How will the EI Smartmiles earned by purchasing the Expo ticket, reflect in my EI SmartMiles account?

It will be credited separately as a bonus in your EI SmartMiles account.

3. How many Valet parking services can I utilize in a month at Expo site?

You can enjoy one complimentary valet parking service per month at Expo site.

4. Is there any spend criteria to utilize the Valet parking service?

Yes, you need to meet the minimum spend threshold mentioned above. If not, the offer will not be applicable, and your credit card will be charged for the availed amount.
For Example:

  • Scenario 1: If you have availed the valet service for the month of September 2021 and have made a total spend of AED 3,000 or more for the same month(September 2021); then you are eligible for the offer and no charges will be levied.
  • Scenario 2: If you have availed the valet service for the month of September 2021 and have made a total spend of AED 2,000 only for the same month(September 2021); then you will be charged with the respective location charges.

5. From when will the Expo site Valet parking benefit can be utilized?

Valet parking benefit will be applicable only from the Expo 2020 event, starting 1 October'2021 to 30 March'2022.

1. Am I eligible for this card if I do not have an U By Emaar membership?

Yes, you are elibile for the card even if you are not an existing U By Emaar member.

2. How will I get enrolled to the U By Emaar Gold Tier status?

You will need to follow the below simple steps to enroll for the U By Emaar Gold Tier status.

  • Step 1: Visit www.eismartmiles.ae, click on 'Activate' and enter your loyalty account number, Date of Birth and Name on your Credit card.
  • Step 2: Activate your account by setting up your login id and password.
  • Step 3: On first login, you will get a pop-up for U By Emaar loyalty programme enrollment.
    • If you are a U By Emaar member, enter your U By Emaar loyalty number and the required fields and click submit.
    • If you are not an U By Emaar member, enter the required fields and click submit.
    • With 48 hours you will receive a confirmation email from Emaar on the loyalty number and the Gold Tier enrollment.

3. When will I earn the bonus 5,500 Upoints?

Welcome bonus of up to 5,500 Upoints will be awarded upon on meeting the below criteria

Card Type Upoints Awarded Eligibility Criteria
Upfront Upoints 3,000 On completion of U By Emaar enrollment through EI SmartMiles portal
Spend Based Upoints 2,500 On doing a retail spend of AED 30,000/- within 90 days from card open date

4. What happens if I don't enroll to U by Emaar through EI SmartMiles portal/app?

If you don't enroll to U By Emaar loyalty programme through EI SmartMiles portal/app, then you will not earn the upfront Upoints and the Gold tier status.

5. What if I don't meet the spend criteria?

If you don't meet the spend criteria within the stipulated time, then you will not be eligible for the Spend based 2,500 Upoints.

1. Is there a spend requirement to avail the movie offer?

Yes, the minimum spend amount mentioned in the above table needs to be met. If not, the offer will not be applicable, and your credit card will be charged for the availed amount.

For example,

  • Scenario 1: If a Expo cardholder books Reel Cinema movie tickets in September 2021 and their total spend for the month of September 2021 was AED 3,000 or more; then they will be eligible for the offer.
  • Scenario 2: If a Expo cardholder books Reel Cinema movie tickets in September 2021 and their total spend for the month of September 2021 was AED 2,000 then they will not be eligible for the offer and the tickets will be charged on their card.

2. What happens if I exceed the maximum discounted tickets usage per month?

If you exceed the maximum complimentary ticket usage then your credit card will be charged with the excess usage charges.

3. Is this offer applicable on both primary & supplementary cards?

Yes, Supplementary Card can also avail the offer. However please note, the maximum complimentary tickets per month is including primary & supplementary cards.

For example,

  • Scenario 1: If Primary Expo cardholder books 2 tickets and Supplementary Expo cardholder books 1 more ticket then it will be considered as 3 complimentary tickets utilized for the respective month. No excess charges will be levied as it is within the max. complimentary tickets per month limit.
  • Scenario 2: If Primary Expo cardholder books 4 tickets and Supplementary Expo cardholder books 2 more tickets then it will be considered as 6 complimentary tickets utilized for the respective month and customer's card account will be charged for the excess usage of 3 tickets.

1. Who is the service provider for Airport Lounge benefit?

The Airport Lounge benefit is offered through LoungeKey, for MasterCard World cardholders.

2. Which are the Airport Lounges that offer complimentary access?

MasterCard World Cardholders can access over 1000+ Lounges worldwide. To view the list of Airports, visit https://www.mastercard.ae/en-ae/consumers/lounge-world.html.

3. Is there any spend requirement to avail the Airport Lounge offer?

There is no spend requirement to avail the Complimentary Airport Lounge access.

4. How long is the complimentary Airport Lounge benefit valid?

The benefit becomes valid upon activation of the card and will stay valid until the card is cancelled or expires.
