Transfer money from your accounts within the UAE and around the world

With Emirates Islamic, you can easily transfer money from your accounts within the UAE or around the world using our channels at your convenience.

Online and Mobile Banking Channels

With our state of the art Online and Mobile banking platform, send money to anywhere in the world from convenience of your home, office or on the go.

Features

  • Available for both Local and International transfers
  • Available transaction amount up to AED 300,000 daily
  • Instant beneficiary registration online
  • Place Standing instructions for recurring transfers
  • Same day processing for Domestic transfers up to 5:00pm and for International transfer requests up to 2:00pm
  • Next business day processing for transfers made after above cut off and on Public Holidays

Frequently Asked Questions

Money Transfer is a remittance product which allows customers to transfer money locally and internationally in any remitting currency via Swift.

You can use EI channels; Branches, Online Banking, and ATMs.

The prevailing exchange rate will be displayed on Online Banking or businessONLINE at the time of performing the transaction. At the branches, the staff will advise the clients the applicable rate of the selected currency.

Special rates are subject to meeting the minimum threshold amount. The minimum threshold amount to obtain a special rate approval for USD/AED is AED 500,000. For other than USD/AED the minimum threshold amount is AED 20,000.

Priority Banking and Business Banking clients will need to request their respective Relationship Managers to get the special rate for their transactions.

Personal banking clients can request the Head Teller in Branches to obtain a special rate.

Information depends upon the beneficiary country. In general, account number/IBAN & SWIFT codes are mandatory for all.

Cut off times varies depending on the currency and the channel used.

For Money Transfers conducted via Online Banking and BusinessOnline

  • BusinessOnline
    • AED (UAE Dirham) – 03:30 pm
    • USD/CAD (US Dollar/ Canadian Dollar) – 04:00 pm
    • GBP/EUR/CHF (Great Britain Pounds, EURO, Swiss Francs) – 03:00 pm
    • Other Currencies – 02:00 pm
  • Online/Mobile Banking
    • For domestic transfers the cutoff time is 5pm
    • For international transfers it's 2pm

For Money Transfers submitted at the Branch: Cut off time is 2:30pm for domestic and international.

If the Money Transfer request is submitted prior to the cut off times, transaction requests will be processed the same day.

Note: If the Money Transfer request is submitted post the cut off times, prevailing exchange rate of the next working day will apply.

You can remit funds to any country except Sanctioned countries.

Online / Mobile Banking Channels:
Local Transfer - up to AED 300K or equivalent
International Transfer - up to AED 300K or equivalent
Between customer's own accounts AED to AED – No Limits
Between customer's own accounts Foreign Currency (non AED or USD) - equivalent to AED 60K

There is no limit for requests submitted in the Branches.

Money Transfer Application which contains all mandatory information will be processed on the same day. Value date of the Transfer is based on the currency cutoff time, and public holidays.

It takes up to 2 to 3 working days as a turnaround time for processing Money Transfers. However, incomplete applications or requests which require further details would be processed after obtaining the required details.

Remitters have the option to choose who shall claim Money Transfer charges including Emirates Islamic, Correspondent and Beneficiary Bank Charges.

Beneficiary ("Receiver") will be liable to pay all related charges if Remitters ("Sender") selects "BEN" or "Beneficiary" option. The amount will be deducted from the remitting amount.

Remitter ("Sender") will pay all said charges if he/she selects "OUR" " or "Applicant".

Charges will be shared with the Remitter ("Sender") and Beneficiary ("Receiver") if charge code "SHA" or "Shared" is selected.

Selecting charge code OUR will ensure that recipients shall receive the entire amount except for countries who do not have a charge-back system such as United States of America (USA).

As per the US banking practice, payments sent with charge Code "OUR" are re-classified into charge code "SHA" as in there is a fee deduction when funds are transferred between banks in the US. This is a not a duplicate charge; the Beneficiary ("Receiver") may expect an additional deduction from the funds received for settlement of any pending correspondent(s) bank charge(s). The charges may vary depending upon the fees and charges of the respective correspondent bank(s).

A confirmation will be sent from Emirates Islamic to the registered Remitter's email address once the amount is debited from the account.

Customers are required to ensure accuracy of details provided prior to submitting the transfer request. If customers would like to stop or cancel any transfer sent then he/she can contact the Emirates Islamic call center to raise the cancelation request. Exchange loss amount, if any, shall be levied in such cases.

Customers are able to raise a standing order request for future/ recurring transfers based on their choice. To setup a future dated Money Transfer or recurring Money Transfer, you can either visit your nearest branch as per your convenience or perform the same via Online Banking. Applicable charges vary based on the customer segment. Please refer to the Schedule of charges for more details.

For standing order request submitted in branches, customers are required to visit the branch and submit the standing order amendment form for amendment requests.

For standing orders scheduled via Online banking, customers need to login to Online Banking > Select the Transfer option > Select Standing Order, Amendment option will be displayed against each scheduled Standing order.
